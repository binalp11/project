Species Locator

To summarize landings for an individual species the species name that is entered in the query must be identical to the name in a master species coding table (names are not case sensitive). The quickest method for selecting and entering a species name is to type part of the name in the box to the left of the SEARCH button. For instance, type (enter) "scallop" and then click on the search button or hit the ENTER key. In the lower box the name "SCALLOP, ATLANTIC CALICO" will appear. To see a complete list of all scallops, click on the down-arrow icon in this box. Scroll down the list of scallop species and pick (click on) the species of your choice to highlight it. Then click on the RETURN button to select this species and return you to your landings query page.

If you want to list all 715 species names, click on the SEARCH button before entering a name in the top box. "ABALONE" should appear in the lower box next to the RETURN button. Click on the down-arrow icon in the box and a scrollable list of NMFS common species names will appear. Choose (click on) the species of your choice and then click on the RETURN button to enter the species in your query and return to the landings query page.
